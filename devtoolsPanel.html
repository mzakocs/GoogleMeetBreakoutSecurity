Google Meet Breakouts

Step 1: Wait for the "Join a breakout room" prompt to appear.

Step 2: Open DevTools and then this panel. Don't open it before the prompt appears.

Step 3: Press the "Join" button to join the breakout room.

Step 4: Once in your assigned breakout room, press the refresh button below.

Step 5: All breakout rooms should be listed below. Click on any to join.

Step 6: After selecting a breakout room, fully join it and wait for the "Join breakout room" prompt to appear. This pops up because you are not in your assigned breakout room. To get rid of this popup, simply press the button below. (Note: This button can also be used to stay in the main meeting when breakout rooms first begin.)

Step 7: Done. You are now in a breakout room you are not supposed to be in. To join a different breakout room, begin again at Step 5.
